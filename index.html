Exploring the Atmosphere's Hidden Microbiome

BROADN (Biology Integration Institute Regional OneHealth Aerobiome Discovery Network) is an NSF-funded initiative at Colorado State University investigating airborne microbial communities across diverse ecosystems. Our OneHealth mission connects atmospheric science, ecology, and public health.
